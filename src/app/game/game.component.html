How to Play

1. Press Start to launch the game.
2. Calculate the sum of numbers and enter you answer in the blank field .
3. Each round has a time limit of 10 seconds, answer entered after 10 seconds even if correct will be marked incorrect.
4. Press End & View Results to view your results. (Result can be view only once game is started)
